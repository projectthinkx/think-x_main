About Think-X

Founded in March 2025 and led by Govind, Dineshbabu, and Srivatsan, Think-X is a remote software company based in Madurai, India. We specialize in innovative, responsive websites, mobile UI/UX, and PC setups, mainly serving startups. Our mission is to deliver cutting-edge digital solutions with a focus on innovation, customer satisfaction, and teamwork. Committed to eco-friendly practices, we have completed numerous successful projects and aim to be a leading tech force. Contact us at think.as.x@gmail.com or visit think-x.in to learn more.

Our team consists of passionate technologists, creative designers, and experienced engineers dedicated to helping businesses grow in the digital era. We believe in continuous learning, adapting to new technologies, and delivering value-driven results for our clients.

At Think-X, we prioritize security, scalability, and user experience in every project. Whether you are a startup or an enterprise, we partner with you to turn your vision into reality through robust software, secure infrastructure, and innovative digital products.
